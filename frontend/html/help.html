Help

How does LuckyGFX work?

LuckyGFX monitors the 1H timeframe for your configured pairs and flags candlestick patterns that match your strategy guidelines. Each alert is validated by a secondary confluence, helping you focus on high-quality setups.

What candlestick pattern is tracked?

The monitored pattern is defined by your strategy admin. For launch, we focus on an engulfing setup with confirmations such as moving averages, RSI divergence, or pivot interactions. Pattern adjustments will be exposed once backend configuration is ready.

How do I troubleshoot missing alerts?

First confirm your monitoring toggle is active on the dashboard. Ensure your phone number is up to date in Settings. If the issue persists, capture the pair and timestamp and share it via the support channel (coming soon).
